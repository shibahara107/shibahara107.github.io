Yoshinori Shibahara / shibahara107

Born in Tokyo, Japan in 2002, Yoshinori Shibahara(=shibahara107) is an artist, programmer, and high school student. After living in Chicago, Illinois (USA) for five years in his elementary school years, Shibahara attended junior high/high school at Shibuya Junior & High School. It was at the age of 12 where he was introduced to programming, and since then has been creating iOS applications. His main interest however lies in art and visuals. Shibahara's theory of combining the fields of art, media, design and technology, along with his unique perspectives on time and human emotions, define Shibahara's creations.
